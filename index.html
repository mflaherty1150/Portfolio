A little background about me

Everything I have done professionally has customer service as a priority. From delivering newspapers as a young boy to waiting tables as a young man. All of my adult work experiences in every aspect of the IT realm, including my time in the travel industry, have always required a strong customer service base. Even with my time in the military, customer service and attention to details was always a specific priority.

I am a thorough and detail oriented problem solver, transitioning from insurance adjusting and IT network support to .NET development. With over 25 years in the IT/network environment and over 4 years in the field of insurance adjusting. I have worked independently as a contractor as well as oversesen a team of over 50 subordinate IT personnel from across the United States, Canada, Mexico, Ghana, and Australia.

I have been "dabbling" in coding since HTML was new! I have finally figured out that this is really what my true calling is. As a developer with this kind of background it has prepared me to think outside the box to develop innovative and creative solutions to a wide spectrum of challenges. I am excited to see where the next chapter of this professional journey takes me.
